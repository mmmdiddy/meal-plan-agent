7-DAY MEAL PLAN
========================================

MONDAY
  Breakfast: Greek Yogurt with Honey and Walnuts
  Lunch:     Vegan Pad Thai with Tofu
  Dinner:    Eggplant Parmesan with Garlic Bread

TUESDAY
  Breakfast: Vegan Tofu Scramble with Avocado Toast
  Lunch:     Caprese Salad with Fresh Mozzarella and Basil
  Dinner:    Vegan Black Bean and Sweet Potato Enchiladas

WEDNESDAY
  Breakfast: Spinach and Feta Omelet with Toast
  Lunch:     Vegan Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice
  Dinner:    Spaghetti Carbonara with Eggs and Parmesan

THURSDAY
  Breakfast: Vegan Chia Pudding with Berries and Almonds
  Lunch:     Greek Salad with Feta and Olives
  Dinner:    Vegan Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa

FRIDAY
  Breakfast: Ricotta Pancakes with Maple Syrup
  Lunch:     Vegan Tom Kha Soup with Coconut Milk
  Dinner:    Cheese Quesadillas with Guacamole and Salsa

SATURDAY
  Breakfast: Vegan Smoothie Bowl with Granola
  Lunch:     Margherita Pizza with Fresh Basil
  Dinner:    Vegan Thai Basil Stir-fry with Brown Rice

SUNDAY
  Breakfast: Greek Frittata with Vegetables
  Lunch:     Vegan Mexican Buddha Bowl with Cilantro Lime Dressing
  Dinner:    Lasagna with Ricotta and Spinach


RECIPES
========================================

MONDAY

  BREAKFAST: Greek Yogurt with Honey and Walnuts
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 cup Greek yogurt
    - 2 tablespoons honey
    - 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  Instructions:
    1. Place Greek yogurt in a serving bowl
    2. Drizzle honey over the yogurt
    3. Sprinkle chopped walnuts on top
    4. Serve immediately

  LUNCH: Vegan Pad Thai with Tofu
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 8 oz rice noodles
    - 14 oz firm tofu, cubed
    - 3 tbsp vegetable oil
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced
    - 2 tbsp tamarind paste
    - 3 tbsp soy sauce
    - 2 tbsp brown sugar
    - 1 tbsp rice vinegar
    - 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
    - 2 cups bean sprouts
    - 3 green onions, chopped
    - 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
    - 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
    - 1 lime, cut into wedges
  Instructions:
    1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft, then drain
    2. Press tofu to remove excess water, then cut into cubes
    3. Heat 2 tbsp oil in large pan or wok over medium-high heat
    4. Add tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides, remove and set aside
    5. Add remaining oil to pan, then add garlic and cook for 30 seconds
    6. Whisk together tamarind paste, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, and red pepper flakes
    7. Add drained noodles to pan and stir-fry for 2 minutes
    8. Pour sauce over noodles and toss to coat
    9. Add cooked tofu back to pan along with bean sprouts
    10. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until bean sprouts are slightly softened
    11. Remove from heat and garnish with green onions, peanuts, and cilantro
    12. Serve immediately with lime wedges

  DINNER: Eggplant Parmesan with Garlic Bread
  Calories: 650 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 2 large eggplants
    - 2 cups all-purpose flour
    - 4 large eggs
    - 3 cups Italian breadcrumbs
    - 2 cups marinara sauce
    - 16 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
    - 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    - 1/2 cup olive oil
    - 1 French bread loaf
    - 4 cloves garlic, minced
    - 4 tbsp butter
    - 2 tbsp fresh parsley
    - Salt and pepper to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Slice eggplants into 1/2 inch rounds and salt both sides, let sit 30 minutes then pat dry
    2. Set up breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs in separate dishes
    3. Dredge eggplant slices in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs
    4. Heat olive oil in large skillet and fry eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides
    5. Preheat oven to 375°F
    6. Spread thin layer of marinara sauce in baking dish
    7. Layer half the eggplant, half remaining sauce, half mozzarella, and half Parmesan
    8. Repeat layers ending with cheese on top
    9. Bake 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden
    10. For garlic bread: mix softened butter, minced garlic, and parsley
    11. Slice French bread and spread with garlic butter mixture
    12. Wrap in foil and bake alongside eggplant for last 10-12 minutes
    13. Let eggplant rest 10 minutes before serving with garlic bread

TUESDAY

  BREAKFAST: Vegan Tofu Scramble with Avocado Toast
  Calories: 520 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 14 oz firm tofu
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
    - 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
    - 1/2 tsp turmeric
    - 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    - 1/4 tsp black pepper
    - 1/2 tsp salt
    - 1/4 cup diced onion
    - 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
    - 2 slices whole grain bread
    - 1 large ripe avocado
    - 1 tbsp lemon juice
    - pinch of salt for avocado
  Instructions:
    1. Drain and crumble tofu into bite-sized pieces
    2. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
    3. Sauté onion and bell pepper for 3-4 minutes until softened
    4. Add crumbled tofu to the pan
    5. Sprinkle turmeric, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, salt, and pepper over tofu
    6. Cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally until heated through and golden
    7. Toast bread slices until golden brown
    8. Mash avocado with lemon juice and pinch of salt
    9. Spread mashed avocado on toast
    10. Serve tofu scramble alongside avocado toast

  LUNCH: Caprese Salad with Fresh Mozzarella and Basil
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 4 large ripe tomatoes
    - 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
    - 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
    - 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    - 1/2 teaspoon salt
    - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  Instructions:
    1. Wash and slice tomatoes into 1/4-inch thick rounds
    2. Slice fresh mozzarella into 1/4-inch thick rounds
    3. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices alternately on a serving platter
    4. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices
    5. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar evenly over the salad
    6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
    7. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld

  DINNER: Vegan Black Bean and Sweet Potato Enchiladas
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
    - 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
    - 8 corn tortillas
    - 1 cup enchilada sauce
    - 1/2 cup diced onion
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 bell pepper, diced
    - 1 tsp cumin
    - 1 tsp chili powder
    - 1/2 tsp paprika
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
    - 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    - 1/4 cup vegan cheese shreds (optional)
  Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 400°F
    2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
    3. Add diced sweet potatoes and cook for 8-10 minutes until tender
    4. Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper, cook for 3-4 minutes
    5. Add black beans, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt and pepper, cook for 2-3 minutes
    6. Remove from heat and stir in cilantro
    7. Warm tortillas in microwave for 30 seconds to make them pliable
    8. Spread 1/4 cup enchilada sauce in bottom of baking dish
    9. Fill each tortilla with 1/3 cup filling, roll tightly and place seam-side down in dish
    10. Pour remaining enchilada sauce over rolled tortillas
    11. Sprinkle with vegan cheese if using
    12. Cover with foil and bake for 20-25 minutes
    13. Remove foil and bake 5 more minutes until heated through
    14. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving

WEDNESDAY

  BREAKFAST: Spinach and Feta Omelet with Toast
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 3 large eggs
    - 2 cups fresh spinach
    - 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - 2 slices bread
    - 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - Salt and pepper to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Toast 2 slices of bread until golden brown
    2. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat
    3. Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes
    4. Beat eggs with salt and pepper in a bowl
    5. Heat butter in the same pan over medium-low heat
    6. Pour in beaten eggs and let set for 30 seconds
    7. Add cooked spinach and feta cheese to one half of the omelet
    8. Fold omelet in half and slide onto plate
    9. Serve immediately with toast

  LUNCH: Vegan Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 cup jasmine rice
    - 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
    - 2-3 tbsp green curry paste
    - 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, cubed
    - 1 Thai eggplant, sliced
    - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    - 1 cup green beans, trimmed
    - 1 onion, sliced
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 inch fresh ginger, minced
    - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    - 1 tbsp soy sauce
    - 1 tbsp brown sugar
    - 1 lime, juiced
    - 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
    - 2 cups vegetable broth
    - Salt to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Rinse jasmine rice until water runs clear, then cook according to package directions
    2. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat
    3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides, then remove and set aside
    4. In the same pan, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant
    5. Add green curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes
    6. Pour in coconut milk and vegetable broth, bring to a simmer
    7. Add eggplant, bell pepper, and green beans, cook for 5-7 minutes
    8. Return tofu to the pan and add soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice
    9. Simmer for 5 more minutes until vegetables are tender
    10. Season with salt to taste and stir in fresh basil leaves
    11. Serve hot over jasmine rice

  DINNER: Spaghetti Carbonara with Eggs and Parmesan
  Calories: 650 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 12 oz spaghetti
    - 4 large eggs
    - 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
    - 4 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/4 cup dry white wine
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    - Salt to taste
    - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  Instructions:
    1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente, reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining
    2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and cook pancetta until crispy, about 4-5 minutes
    3. Add minced garlic to pancetta and cook for 1 minute until fragrant
    4. Add white wine to deglaze the pan and cook until mostly evaporated
    5. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper
    6. Add hot drained pasta to the skillet with pancetta
    7. Remove skillet from heat and quickly toss pasta with egg mixture, adding pasta water gradually until creamy
    8. Season with salt and pepper to taste
    9. Serve immediately topped with extra Parmesan and fresh parsley

THURSDAY

  BREAKFAST: Vegan Chia Pudding with Berries and Almonds
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1/4 cup chia seeds
    - 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
    - 2 tablespoons maple syrup
    - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    - 1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
    - 1/4 cup sliced almonds
    - pinch of salt
  Instructions:
    1. In a bowl, whisk together chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined
    2. Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes, then whisk again to prevent clumping
    3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight until thick and pudding-like
    4. Stir the pudding before serving
    5. Divide into serving bowls and top with fresh berries and sliced almonds

  LUNCH: Greek Salad with Feta and Olives
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 4 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
    - 1 cucumber, sliced
    - 1 red onion, thinly sliced
    - 1 green bell pepper, sliced
    - 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
    - 4 oz feta cheese, cubed
    - 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
    - 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    - 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    - Salt and pepper to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Cut tomatoes into wedges and place in a large bowl
    2. Add sliced cucumber, red onion, and bell pepper to the bowl
    3. Add Kalamata olives and cubed feta cheese
    4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper
    5. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine
    6. Let salad sit for 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld
    7. Serve at room temperature or chilled

  DINNER: Vegan Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa
  Calories: 285 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 4 large bell peppers
    - 1 cup quinoa
    - 2 cups vegetable broth
    - 1 medium onion diced
    - 2 cloves garlic minced
    - 1 can diced tomatoes
    - 1 cup corn kernels
    - 1/2 cup black beans
    - 1 tsp cumin
    - 1 tsp paprika
    - 1/2 tsp chili powder
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
    - 1/4 cup fresh cilantro chopped
  Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F
    2. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes
    3. Rinse quinoa and cook in vegetable broth for 15 minutes until fluffy
    4. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion until translucent
    5. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
    6. Add diced tomatoes, corn, black beans, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt and pepper
    7. Cook mixture for 5-7 minutes
    8. Stir in cooked quinoa and cilantro
    9. Stuff bell peppers with quinoa mixture
    10. Place stuffed peppers in baking dish with 1/4 cup water at bottom
    11. Cover with foil and bake for 35-40 minutes
    12. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes until peppers are tender

FRIDAY

  BREAKFAST: Ricotta Pancakes with Maple Syrup
  Calories: 380 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 cup ricotta cheese
    - 3 large eggs
    - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    - 2 tablespoons sugar
    - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    - 1/4 teaspoon salt
    - 2 tablespoons butter
    - maple syrup for serving
  Instructions:
    1. In a bowl, whisk together ricotta cheese, eggs, flour, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth
    2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat
    3. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake into the pan
    4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on surface and edges look set
    5. Flip carefully and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown
    6. Serve immediately with maple syrup

  LUNCH: Vegan Tom Kha Soup with Coconut Milk
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 2 cups coconut milk
    - 2 cups vegetable broth
    - 3 stalks lemongrass (bruised and chopped)
    - 4 kaffir lime leaves
    - 3 Thai chilies (sliced)
    - 2-inch piece galangal (sliced)
    - 8 oz mushrooms (sliced)
    - 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
    - 3 tbsp lime juice
    - 2 tbsp soy sauce
    - 1 tsp palm sugar
    - 1/4 cup cilantro (chopped)
    - 2 green onions (sliced)
  Instructions:
    1. Heat vegetable broth in a large pot over medium heat
    2. Add lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, and Thai chilies to the broth
    3. Simmer for 10 minutes to infuse flavors
    4. Add coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer
    5. Add mushrooms and bell pepper, cook for 5 minutes until tender
    6. Stir in lime juice, soy sauce, and palm sugar
    7. Remove from heat and strain out the aromatics if desired
    8. Garnish with cilantro and green onions before serving

  DINNER: Cheese Quesadillas with Guacamole and Salsa
  Calories: 650 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 4 large flour tortillas
    - 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
    - 2 ripe avocados
    - 1 lime (juiced)
    - 1/4 red onion (diced)
    - 1 jalapeño (seeded and minced)
    - 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    - Salt to taste
    - 2 medium tomatoes (diced)
    - 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  Instructions:
    1. Make guacamole by mashing avocados in a bowl, then mix in half the lime juice, half the diced onion, jalapeño, 1 minced garlic clove, salt, and half the cilantro
    2. Make salsa by combining diced tomatoes, remaining onion, remaining garlic, remaining lime juice, remaining cilantro, and salt in a bowl
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
    4. Place 1 tortilla in skillet and sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese evenly over half the tortilla
    5. Fold tortilla in half and cook 2-3 minutes until golden brown
    6. Flip quesadilla and cook another 2-3 minutes until cheese melts and other side is golden
    7. Remove from skillet and repeat with remaining tortillas and cheese
    8. Cut each quesadilla into wedges and serve with guacamole and salsa

SATURDAY

  BREAKFAST: Vegan Smoothie Bowl with Granola
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 frozen banana
    - 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
    - 1/4 cup coconut milk
    - 1 tbsp chia seeds
    - 1/2 cup rolled oats
    - 2 tbsp maple syrup
    - 1/4 cup chopped almonds
    - 2 tbsp coconut flakes
    - 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
    - 1/4 tsp cinnamon
    - fresh berries for topping
  Instructions:
    1. Make granola by mixing oats, chopped almonds, coconut flakes, 1 tbsp maple syrup, vanilla, and cinnamon, then bake at 300°F for 15-20 minutes until golden
    2. Blend frozen banana, frozen berries, coconut milk, and remaining maple syrup until thick and creamy
    3. Pour smoothie into a bowl
    4. Top with homemade granola, chia seeds, and fresh berries
    5. Serve immediately

  LUNCH: Margherita Pizza with Fresh Basil
  Calories: 320 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 pizza dough ball
    - 1/2 cup pizza sauce
    - 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
    - Salt to taste
    - Black pepper to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C)
    2. Roll out pizza dough on floured surface to 12-inch circle
    3. Transfer dough to pizza stone or baking sheet
    4. Brush dough with 1 tablespoon olive oil
    5. Spread pizza sauce evenly over dough, leaving 1-inch border
    6. Arrange mozzarella slices evenly over sauce
    7. Season with salt and pepper
    8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly
    9. Remove from oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves
    10. Drizzle with remaining olive oil
    11. Let cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing

  DINNER: Vegan Thai Basil Stir-fry with Brown Rice
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 cup brown rice
    - 2 cups water
    - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    - 3 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1 small onion, sliced
    - 1 bell pepper, sliced
    - 1 cup broccoli florets
    - 1 cup snap peas
    - 2 tbsp soy sauce
    - 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
    - 1 tsp sugar
    - 1/4 tsp white pepper
    - 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
    - 2 Thai chilies, sliced
    - 2 tbsp vegetable broth
  Instructions:
    1. Cook brown rice with water in a pot, bring to boil then simmer covered for 45 minutes until tender
    2. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat
    3. Add garlic and chilies, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant
    4. Add onion and bell pepper, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
    5. Add broccoli and snap peas, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp-tender
    6. Mix soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, white pepper and vegetable broth in a small bowl
    7. Pour sauce over vegetables and toss to coat
    8. Add Thai basil leaves and stir-fry for 1 minute until wilted
    9. Serve immediately over cooked brown rice

SUNDAY

  BREAKFAST: Greek Frittata with Vegetables
  Calories: 285 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 8 large eggs
    - 1/4 cup milk
    - 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
    - 1 medium zucchini, diced
    - 1 red bell pepper, diced
    - 1/2 red onion, diced
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    - 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil
    - 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
    - 2 tablespoons fresh oregano
    - Salt and pepper to taste
  Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
    2. Heat olive oil in a 10-inch oven-safe skillet over medium heat
    3. Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened
    4. Add bell pepper and zucchini, cook for 5-6 minutes until tender
    5. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant
    6. Add cherry tomatoes and olives, cook for 2 minutes
    7. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper
    8. Stir in dill, oregano, and half the feta cheese
    9. Pour egg mixture over vegetables in the skillet
    10. Sprinkle remaining feta cheese on top
    11. Cook on stovetop for 2-3 minutes until edges begin to set
    12. Transfer skillet to oven and bake for 12-15 minutes until center is set and top is lightly golden
    13. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving

  LUNCH: Vegan Mexican Buddha Bowl with Cilantro Lime Dressing
  Calories: 485 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 1 cup quinoa
    - 1 can black beans (15 oz), drained and rinsed
    - 1 cup corn kernels
    - 1 red bell pepper, diced
    - 1 avocado, sliced
    - 2 cups mixed greens
    - 1/4 cup red onion, diced
    - 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
    - 2 limes, juiced
    - 3 tbsp olive oil
    - 1 clove garlic, minced
    - 1 tsp cumin
    - Salt and pepper to taste
    - 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  Instructions:
    1. Cook quinoa according to package directions and let cool
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, minced garlic, cumin, salt and pepper to make dressing
    3. Heat corn in a pan for 2-3 minutes until lightly charred
    4. Season black beans with cumin, salt and pepper
    5. Arrange mixed greens in bowls as base
    6. Top with quinoa, black beans, corn, diced bell pepper, and red onion
    7. Add sliced avocado and sprinkle with cilantro and pumpkin seeds
    8. Drizzle with cilantro lime dressing and serve immediately

  DINNER: Lasagna with Ricotta and Spinach
  Calories: 380 kcal
  Ingredients:
    - 12 lasagna noodles
    - 2 cups ricotta cheese
    - 10 oz frozen spinach, thawed and drained
    - 1 egg
    - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 3 cups marinara sauce
    - 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - 1/2 tsp salt
    - 1/4 tsp black pepper
    - 1/4 tsp nutmeg
    - 2 tbsp olive oil
  Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F and cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
    2. Squeeze excess water from thawed spinach and mix with ricotta, egg, garlic, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
    3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the spinach-ricotta mixture for 2-3 minutes
    4. Spread 1/2 cup marinara sauce in bottom of 9x13 baking dish
    5. Layer 4 noodles, 1/3 of ricotta-spinach mixture, 1/3 of remaining sauce, and 1/3 of mozzarella
    6. Repeat layers twice more
    7. Top with Parmesan cheese and cover with foil
    8. Bake for 25 minutes, remove foil, and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly
    9. Let rest 10 minutes before serving
